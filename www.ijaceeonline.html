About this Journal

International Journal of Advances in Computer and Electronics Engineering (IJACEE) is a monthly peer-reviewed open access journal. IJACEE publishes research articles, review articles, short communications, letters and technical notes in the following research areas (but not limited to). Students, research scholars, academicians, and industrialists are invited to publish their research works with this journal to disseminate the research ideas around the globe.
